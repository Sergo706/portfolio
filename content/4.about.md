#intro
I’m a fullstack engineer working in the TypeScript ecosystem, focused on building reliable systems that operate correctly under real-world conditions. My interest in engineering sits at the intersection of backend architecture, distributed systems, and defensive design. I enjoy working close to the fundamentals of how systems behave on different constraints, request flows, state management, failure modes, and the small implementation details that determine whether software holds up under pressure. Before transitioning into engineering, I spent several years leading operations for a large hospitality business, managing teams, logistics, and risk in fast moving environments. That experience shaped how I approach software, systems should be predictable, observable, and resilient because real users and real businesses depend on them.
